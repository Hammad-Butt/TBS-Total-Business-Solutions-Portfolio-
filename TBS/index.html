ABOUT US

Total Business Solutions (TBS) is a result-oriented, professional consulting company. TBS has a 100% success rate in helping customers complete system certifications like QMS (Quality Management System - ISO 9001), EMS (Environmental Management System - ISO 14001), HSMS (Health & Safety Management System - OHSAS 18001), FSMS (Food Safety Management System - ISO 22000), and compliance certifications like WRAP, SA 8000, BSCI, Sedex, and Buyers Code of Conduct trainings, within predetermined time limits and with the highest level of quality and customer satisfaction.

TBS is committed to working with your organization to bring you the most effective and efficient solutions to your training needs and other related services.

TBS is the leading Management System Consultancy, Training, Certification, HR/Recruitment Services Provider Organization, offering the services of its qualified, experienced, and competent consultants, trainers, and auditors to help organizations improve the efficiency and effectiveness of their processes and activities to stay competitive and profitable.
